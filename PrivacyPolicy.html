My privacy policy

I am not spying on you, though it's hard to know.

Ever wonder why you can use Google, Facebook, YouTube, and (maybe outside the US) TikTok for free? But we pay for movies and articles? The sites mentioned in the former clause make money by collecting user data. And by allowing advertisers to use that data to sell to you, the more users spend time on such a site, the better. While internet ads have been hated since the beginning, advertisers have to trust these platforms that their products are being sold. I know why social media sites are popular, though; they are easy to use and provide services for free that we used to pay for.

The thing is, to host websites like this and provide services to users cost money. All websites are hosted on computers that run all day long and people like free things. It's not hard to see why people sacfice privacy for a little convientce is it?

But if you have an idea for a product or service that works on a server that people will pay for, or a basic service that ad revenue could support, I'd like to help you.
