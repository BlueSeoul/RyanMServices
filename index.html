About Me

Hello, I am Ryan McCurdy. I went to school majoring in video game design and later communications. Despite this, I've been teaching English for many years, but I've since rekindled my technical interests and would like to build my skills in creating websites, fixing videos, and creating apps. 

Helping people reach their goals is what drives me, regardless of whether it's a digital service, a video game, or something else. I know these areas of expertise seem far apart, but how many of us are interacting with web pages hosting video files? There are quite a lot of us. Especially for people who use apps.

My service is not working, why? How do I revise a terrible mistake in my YouTube sort? Why can't things work the way I want them to? It's exciting to work out these kinds of questions.
